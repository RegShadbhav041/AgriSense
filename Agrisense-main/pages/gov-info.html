Seasonal Advisory

  • Monsoon: ensure drainage, avoid waterlogging in paddy nurseries.
  • Pre-winter: prepare seed beds for wheat and vegetables.
  • Heat alerts: mulch soil and irrigate during evening to reduce loss.

Pest & Disease Helpline

Dial 1618-00-AGRI or message photos via official agri app. Field staff can schedule visits for severe outbreaks.

Market & MSP Pointers

  • Check weekly wholesale prices at district market boards.
  • MSP announcements are posted at ward offices and AKC notice boards.

Disaster & Relief

Flood/drought relief requires ward verification and crop loss photos. Insurance claim within 72 hours recommended.

Farmer ID & Registration

  • Carry citizenship copy + land paper/lease for registration.
  • Farmer ID eases subsidy, credit, and insurance processing.
