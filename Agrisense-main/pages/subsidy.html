Fertilizer Subsidy

Up to 30-50% subsidy on urea, DAP, and potash through registered cooperatives. Carry citizenship copy and farmer ID.

  • Apply at nearest agri co-op
  • Keep purchase invoice for reimbursement
  • Contact: Local Agriculture Knowledge Center (AKC)

Seed Support

Certified seeds for rice, maize, wheat with 25-40% support.

  • Visit seed distribution counters during season
  • Farmer group recommendation helps priority

Irrigation & Micro-irrigation

Drip/sprinkler kits with up to 60% subsidy; small pump sets for hills/terai.

  • Submit land ownership/lease proof
  • Joint inspection by ward + AKC

Crop Insurance

Premium subsidy up to 75% for major crops and livestock.

  • Insure before sowing for full coverage
  • Report damage within 72 hours
